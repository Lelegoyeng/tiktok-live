    Description

    Bitcoin is the first decentralized cryptocurrency. Nodes in the bitcoin network verify transactions through cryptography and record them in a public distributed ledger called a blockchain. Based on a free market ideology, bitcoin was invented in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto, an unknown person. Wikipedia
    Symbols: BTC, ฿, ₿
    Block reward: ₿6.25 (As of 2023)
    Circulating supply: ₿18,925,000 (As of 10 January 2022)
    Code: BTC, XBT
    Exchange rate: Floating
    Hash function: SHA-256 (two rounds)
    Official user(s): El Salvador
